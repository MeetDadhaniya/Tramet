# Tramet ✈️

### Your Trip, Assembled by AI.

Tramet is an AI-powered travel planning platform that turns the chaos of trip planning into one guided, visual, and conversational experience.

Instead of comparing dozens of tabs, filling out forms, and manually managing budgets, travelers simply tell Tramet their **budget, vibe, travel preferences, and who they’re travelling with**.

Tramet then assembles a complete, editable trip plan.

## ✨ Key Features

* 💬 **Conversational Onboarding** — Plan your trip through a natural, one-question-at-a-time experience.
* 🎨 **Vibe-Based Discovery** — Pick images that match your travel mood instead of using boring checkboxes.
* 🤖 **AI Trip Assembly** — Generate destinations, stays, activities, and itineraries personalized to you.
* ⛽ **Budget Fuel Gauge** — Watch your budget change live as you customize the trip.
* 🗺️ **Map-First Exploration** — Explore destinations and stays through an interactive map.
* 🌍 **Community & Discovery** — Discover posts, photos, and travel stories connected to real locations.
* 🔥 **Heatmap Globe** — See where people are travelling through a living 3D globe.

## 💡 Why Tramet?

Most travel apps either help you **search** or help you **get inspired**.

Tramet does both.

It understands what kind of trip you want, assembles the plan, lets you explore it visually, and keeps everything within your budget.

> **Planning a trip should feel as exciting as taking one.**
